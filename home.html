Our activities

Painting

Painting Competition is a unique drawing, painting and art competition designed to encourage creative expression. Art competitions are the best avenues to show your masterpieces, through which you can finally break the ice and gain the exposure.


Picnic

A school picnic is an event where students go outside with teachers and enjoy the day together. School life is a happy life not because we don't have any career pressure but because we have lots of happy memories. Various events, programs,etc make study.


Sports Day

Sports day is an occasion where pupils, teachers, and parents come together for a day of competitive sport. Some schools choose to offer medals or prizes to the winners of the various events, but for others, participation is its own reward.
